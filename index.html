BRIDGING THE GAP BETWEEN PEOPLE

A glove which converts hand gestures to speech

Our Product

SIGMA is a wearable gadget which interprets hand gestures. It can find a variety of applications in various fields. Our main objective of designing this glove started as an attempt to help those lacking the sense of hearing and ability to speak.

Future Expansions

Gaming Console

This glove can be used to play games by acting as game controller giving a realistic feel

3D Modelling

This glove can be used for 3D modelling

Home Automation

Control home appliances sitting at one place using glove
